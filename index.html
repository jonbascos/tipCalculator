How much of a tip should I leave?

This app will show you how much you should leave depending on the percentage you want to tip. And for those that don't want to do the math, it will also show you what the total bill will be including the tip.

How much is your total bill? $

According to CNN/Money, the tip you should leave depends on the services received. For example, you would tip anywhere between 10% and 20% of your total bill (excluding taxes) depending on if you think you recieved poor service to very good service.
